## What’s new

- Smart now balances Squat, Bench Press and Deadlift frequency across recent training days.
- Overtrained lifts are avoided even when they are the current meet-plan limiter.
- Smart can generate a focused single-lift workout when a second lift would hurt recovery or frequency balance.
- Single-lift Bench Press sessions now include additional appropriate volume.
- Smart workout status information is clearer and more compact.
