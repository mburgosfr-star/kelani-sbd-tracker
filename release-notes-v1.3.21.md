# Kelani SBD Tracker 1.3.21

This release improves Smart workout planning and meet preparation.

## Improvements

- Redesigned the Smart workout details modal for clearer meet-readiness information.
- Clarified the difference between the current-cycle e1RM and the planned meet opener.
- Improved primary lift progression using recent successful training volume.
- Improved meet-specific intensity and back-off selection.
- Reduced back-off sets on mixed-lift meet-preparation workouts.
- Reduced accessory workload on two-lift training days to one important accessory per lift.
- Prevented Smart from prescribing an exact repeat of a recent workout when safe progression is available.

## Reliability

- Added regression coverage for cycle readiness, load progression, accessory selection, and repeated workout prevention.
