# Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2026-06-02

Kelani SBD Tracker is an offline-first powerlifting tracker for Squat, Bench Press and Deadlift training.

The app is built to work without accounts, ads, tracking or analytics.

## Data collection

Kelani SBD Tracker does not collect personal data and does not send training data to the developer or to any server.

The app stores your training data locally on your device. This may include:

- Workout data
- Training history
- Bodyweight and body composition entries
- Meet Planner attempts
- Meet prep checklist state
- App settings such as language and rest time

This data stays on your device unless you choose to export or share it.

## Accounts

Kelani SBD Tracker does not use accounts. You do not need to sign in, register or provide an email address.

## Ads and tracking

Kelani SBD Tracker contains no ads, no analytics and no tracking.

## Data sharing

Kelani SBD Tracker does not share your data with the developer or third parties.

If you use the export or share features, you choose where the exported file goes. That action is controlled by you and may involve other apps on your device.

## Data retention and deletion

Your app data is kept locally on your device.

You can delete your data by clearing the app data, uninstalling the app, or replacing it with an imported backup.

Because Kelani SBD Tracker does not use accounts or servers, the developer does not hold a copy of your app data and cannot delete server-side data.

## Security

Kelani SBD Tracker stores data locally using the storage available on your device. You are responsible for keeping your device and backups secure.
