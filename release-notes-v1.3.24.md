## Improvements

- Smart training now prevents equivalent primary-lift prescriptions from repeating merely because the number of work sets changes.
- Smart lift grids are always completely filled while staying within safe volume limits.
- Meet readiness now distinguishes opener readiness, second-attempt support and third-attempt potential.
- The Smart modal now shows projected meet timing by cycle and workout, the current blocker and the projected limiting lift.
- Smart workout decisions now include clearer progression and volume explanations plus a copyable diagnostic report.

## Validation

- Universal progression coverage for Squat, Bench Press and Deadlift across different strength levels.
- Full automated test suite and visible web test passed.
